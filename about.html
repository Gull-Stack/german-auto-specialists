Meet the Owner

Vincent Gillespie

Vincent — known to his customers as Vinny or Vince — didn't just learn to fix German cars. He grew up fascinated by the precision engineering that makes Audi, BMW, Volkswagen, and Mini stand apart from every other car on the road.

That fascination turned into a career spanning over two decades. After years of honing his skills, Vincent opened German Auto Specialists in West Jordan, Utah, with a simple mission: provide dealership-quality German auto repair with the honesty, personal attention, and fair pricing that only an independent shop can offer.

Today, customers drive from across the Salt Lake Valley — South Jordan, Riverton, Herriman, Murray, Sandy, and beyond — because they know Vincent won't sell them something they don't need, won't cut corners on quality, and will treat their car like it's his own.

"I'd rather lose a sale than lose your trust. If your car doesn't need a repair, I'll tell you. That's how I've built this business — one honest conversation at a time."

— Vincent Gillespie, Owner
