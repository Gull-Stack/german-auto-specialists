Common BMW Issues We Fix Every Week

Cooling system failures are the number one issue with BMWs in Utah. The plastic expansion tanks, water pumps, and thermostats degrade over time, especially in our extreme temperature swings. We replace the entire cooling system proactively — it's cheaper than an overheated engine.

Oil leaks are practically a BMW trademark. Valve cover gaskets, oil filter housing gaskets, and oil pan gaskets all eventually seep. We fix these properly with quality gaskets and correct torque specs — not quick-fix sealants.

VANOS solenoid and timing issues cause rough idle, loss of power, and check engine lights. Our BMW diagnostic tools can read VANOS timing data in real-time to pinpoint the exact failure, saving you from unnecessary part replacements.
